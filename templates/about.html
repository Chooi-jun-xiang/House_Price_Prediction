What is the problem you are facing?

First and foremost, The problem we are facing is accurately predicting house prices in Malaysiawhere we focusing on specific high-demand region which is Kuala Lumpur based on a varietyoffeatures such as location, property size, number of bedrooms, nearby facilities such as schools, hospitals, clinics, transportation, supermarkets and so on. Not only that, Current methods of house pricing may too rely on subjective evaluations or outdated data which will leadto inconsistent or inaccurate pricing. Therefore, Developing a data-driven model will allowfor more reliable, consistent and fair pricing predictions, which are important for both buyers andsellers.

How many people/organizations/places/etc and how much are they affected?

1. Homebuyers: Every year, almost 200,000 Malaysians rely on proper pricing to prevent overspending or the loss of reasonably priced homes. The National Property Information Centre (NAPIC), which keeps track on yearly real estate transactions throughout Malaysia, provided the data used to calculate this number. 2. Sellers: Since overpricing or underpricing their homes can result in financial losses or delays in sales, some 200,000 real estate sellers are also affected. The yearly propertymarket report from NAPIC, which shows the total number of transactions, is another sourceof this number. 3. Real Estate Agents: According to the Board of Valuers, Appraisers, and Estate Agents Malaysia, or BOVAEA, there are over 24,000 registered real estate agents in Malaysia. Theyrely on precise home price to advise their clients and close transactions. Mispricing couldprevent successful deals and harm their professional reputation. 4. Investors: To evaluate possible returns, real estate investors depend on precise home pricing. In high-growth regions, poor pricing forecasts can result in bad investment decisions andfinancial losses, according to reports from Knight Frank and iProperty Malaysia.

Goals

What are the business/policy goals that will be accomplished by solving this problem and what constraints do you have?

Increase the Reliability of Home Price Predictions

Increase Real Estate Market Transparency

Support Decision-Making Processes

Analysis

1. Description: Analyze historical house price trends (location, size, bedrooms)toinform buyers and agents. Validate using historical data to comparepricechangesacross neighborhoods.

2.Prediction: Estimate future house prices based on current data. Validateusingregression models and measure accuracy with metrics like MAEandR-squared.

3.Optimization: Identify optimal property pricing strategies throughA/Btestingandanalyze sales performance. Validate by evaluating conversion ratesandtimeonmarket.
